## What is it?
Spaceflight Simulator is a fun website where people can simulate their very own launch to the ISS!

## Inspiration
I have a strong interest in space exploration. In simulations, a key factor is how realistic the simulation is. In terms of launching a rocket, there are many factors involved. 
Of course, this project is a little bit simplified, but it gives an idea of how even one factor can affect all of the different variables. This is what inspired me to make a 
simulation to launch rockets.

## How it works
There are a variety of different inputs: crew size, type of rocket, cargo weight, and fuel load. Then, when you press 'Initiate launch sequence', the system calculates
the amount of risk involved. If there is too much risk, the launch is aborted. Additionally, there is a randomized system of weather events. In the event of a solar storm
or stormy weather, the launch is also aborted. If there is too little fuel, the launch is aborted. But, if all goes well, the rocket launches, and the mission is a success.
