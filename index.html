Welcome to Spaceflight Simulator

Never launched a rocket before? You are at the right place!

In this simulation, you will adjust different parameters: crew size, cargo weight, spacecraft type, and fuel load (in kg). After you click the "Initiate Launch Sequence" button, the simulation will determine if your mission is successful based on randomized weather activity and the parameters you set and how much risk it creates.
